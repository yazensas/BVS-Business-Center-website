Company Overview

A Professional Workspace, Made Simple

BVS Business Center provides affordable and practical office solutions for businesses in Al Barsha First, Dubai. We specialize in ready-to-use private office spaces ranging from 100 to 345 sq. ft., designed for startups, entrepreneurs, SMEs, and growing companies.

Our offices are created for businesses that need a professional workspace without the high costs and complexity of traditional office leasing. Each office includes essential services such as DEWA, Wi-Fi, air conditioning, shared reception services, drinking water, common area maintenance, and Ejari for every tenant.
